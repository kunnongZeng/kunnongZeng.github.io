Kunnong Zeng

I'm a PhD student at the University of Utah in Salt Lake City, working with Prof. Cem Yuksel and Prof. Erik Brunvand. My research interests lie in rendering, hardware ray tracing and novel view synthesis.

Prior to University of Utah, I acquired my Master and Bachelor degrees from Tsinghua University and University of Electronic Science and Technology of China respectively.
